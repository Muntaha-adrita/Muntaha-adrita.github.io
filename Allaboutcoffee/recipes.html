Recipes

There are a bunch of great forms of coffee drinks and recipes. The 10 most common and popular of them are depicted here. Enjoy!

10 Best Recipes

Espresso

1.Place finely grounded coffee beans in filter basket of coffee maker.
2.Brew with small amount of water.
3.Espresso is ready



Cappuccino

1.Brew a perfect cup of espresso.
2.Add hot milk with foam on it.
3.Garnish with chocolate powder or cream according to your preference.
4.Your Cappuccino is ready.



Americano

1.Brew a perfect cup of espresso.
2.Add hot water according to obtain your perfect strength.
3.Your Americano is ready.



Iced Coffee

1.Soak coffee bean in water over the night.
2.Filter the water next day.
3.Mix it with milk and sweetener.
4.Refrigerate it some for time.
5.Serve with ice.



Mocha

1.Brew coffee. While hot, stir in hot chocolate mix until completely dissolved.
2.Add half-and-half.
3.Top with whipped cream and cocoa sprinkles (optional).



Dalgona

1.Add the instant coffee, sugar and hot water to a medium mixing bowl. Using an electric hand-held mixer, whip the coffee mixture until it is light brown, fluffy and holds stiff peaks when the whisk is removed.
2.Heat the milk, if desired, and divide between two heatproof glasses. Spoon dollops of the frothed coffee mixture on top and smooth out with a spoon. Serve.



Latte

1.Pull your espresso shot in your latte cup and add a shot of flavored syrup for a flavored latte, if using.
2.Using a spoon to retain the microbubbles on top of the steamed milk, pour the bottom 2/3 of the steamed milk from the steaming pitcher into the latte cup.
3.Top the latte with the remaining bubbles.



Affogato

1.Add 2 scoops of ice cream to a serving bowl or glass.
2.Add chocolate shavings or other toppings if desired.
3.Pour 2 shots of hot espresso or strong brewed coffee over the ice cream and serve right away.



Caramel Coffee

1.In small saucepan, stir together milk, caramel sauce and brown sugar. Heat over medium heat 4 to 5 minutes or until foamy and steaming, whisking frequently.
2.Divide coffee between 2 large mugs. Divide milk mixture between mugs and stir. Top each mug with whipped cream and additional caramel sauce.



Irish Coffee

1.Lightly whip the cream just so it’s very slightly thickened, then set aside.
2.Pour the hot coffee into a mug or heatproof glass, then add the whiskey and sugar. Stir until the sugar has dissolved.
3.Gently float the cream on the top and sprinkle the nutmeg over the cream. Serve hot.
